About us

FVM Trade (Fresh Vegetables & Meat) is a family-owned business that works in import and export of fresh vegetables, purees, juice concentrates, and meat. Our second company is a food producer and was established in 1989, since that time we have selected the best farmers, plants and slaughterhouses of pork, poultry, and beef.

We work close with certified suppliers, in order to meet the highest quality standards we check the quality of each

A wide range of top-quality products according to customer requirements, ensuring continuity and flexibility of supplies, as well as full control of the production process in accordance with international standards.

Currently, we cooperate with partners from Europe, however we are open to work with companies from different countries all over the world.


Thanks to many years of cooperation with the best producers, we are attractive to our contractors in terms of price and quality. Majority of our farmers possess Global GAP certificate, and companies that we work with possess IFS, BRC, HALAL certification, it proves the highest quality to meet the quality standards.
